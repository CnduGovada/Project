Tasks

The following tasks were given to the participants and no assistance was provided.

  1. Understand the purpose of website.

  2. To find out more information about the organization.

  3. A check was made to identify the user friendliness of the background color and its acceptability. Also, a synchronization between the background color and images on the website was checked.

  4. Readability of the textual information on a webpage was identified by questioning the users.

  5. Go to registration page and register. A check was made to understand the ease of filling the form by user.

  6. Based upon the background color, some users were asked whether they could easily identify links on web page.

  7. A synchronization between the background color and images on the website is also checked.

  8. To find the feedback page.

    9.

Feedback given by the Participants

  • The purpose of the website is well understood.

  • Able to read the text clearly and can understand the structure of the website.

  • The website color is subtle and pleasing.

  • Text is easily readable and font size is good enough to read.

  • Background color does not intefere with font color.

  • The images are good and loads quickly.

  • Navigation is easy.

  • Registration and Feedback Form contents are clearly visible. Form labels are correctly aligned with details required.

  • Overall Site is very good and useful.
